My hobbies

Sewing

I enjoy sewing because it allows me to create unique clothing and accessories. It's a relaxing and rewarding hobby that combines creativity and practicality. Sewing has always been a creative outlet for me. From designing clothes to crafting unique accessories, I love bringing my ideas to life with fabric and thread.

Acting

Acting is my passion because it enables me to explore various characters and emotions. I enjoy the process of getting into a role, understanding the character's background, and delivering performance. Whether on stage or in front of a camera, acting is an exhilarating experience that challenges me to grow amd improve.

Modeling

Modeling is an empowering hobby that combines fashion and photography. It allows me to work with talented photographers, stylist, and designers to create visuals. Through modelling, I can showcase different styles and trends, and it's always exciting to see the final results of a photoshoot.
